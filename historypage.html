Club Overview

Football Club Barcelona, commonly referred to as FC Barcelona or simply Barcelona, is a professional football club based in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. Founded in 1899 by a group of Swiss, English, and Catalan footballers, the club has become a symbol of Catalan culture and Catalanism

Success and Achievements

FC Barcelona has a rich history of success both domestically and internationally. The club has won numerous La Liga titles, Copa del Rey trophies, and has been highly successful in European competitions, winning multiple UEFA Champions League titles.

club legends

Over the years, FC Barcelona has been home to some of the greatest football players in the world. Legends like Lionel Messi, Xavi Hernandez, and Andres Iniesta have played pivotal roles in the club's success.


fc barcelona Trophies

A handful of clubs in the world can be proud of having won so many, The Club's greatest pride lies in its 5 European Cups won at Wembley (1992, 2011), Paris (2006), Rome (2009), Berlin (2015) and its FIFA Club World Cups (2009, 2011 and 2015)
